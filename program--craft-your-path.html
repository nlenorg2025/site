OUR PROGRAMS

Craft Your Path

Craft Your Path is dedicated to equipping young individuals with the skills and confidence needed for successful employment. Through hands-on training and mentorship, we pave the way for brighter futures in the workforce.

Empowering the next generation

Craft Your Path is designed for young people ages 18-24 who are seeking employment and career development opportunities, and are not in school. The program’s goal is to equip young individuals with essential job skills and readiness, while connecting participants to meaningful employment opportunities, especially in emerging careers in healthcare, hospitality, skilled trades, and transportation, distribution and logistics. Our program focuses on fostering confidence and providing resources for successful employment.

Craft Your Path participants must:

  • Be between the ages of 18-24
  • Not be currently attending school
  • Attend an NLEN information session
  • Commit to our job-readiness program and work-based learning

Program features

Design the path that works for you.

No matter what you've experienced before you walk through our doors, you have the power to Craft Your Path to a bright and prosperous future. We are committed to partnering with you on this journey to help you achieve the vision you have established for yourself, your family, and your community.

Enroll today

Comprehensive Job Readiness

  • Receive essential training to succeed in your job search and in the workplace.

Work-Based Learning

  • You’ll be paired with paid work-based learning experiences that complement the skills you’ll learn in our classrooms, to help build your professional networks.

Mentorship

  • Professional mentorship and coaching to support your career path and professional goals.

Wraparound Services

  • Connections to essential support services including housing support, child care assistance programs, and nutrition support programs.

$8,000 Education Award

  • Each participant receives an $8,000 education award, which can be used to obtain key training credentials at local community colleges and trade schools.

Access to our Alumni Network

  • Connect with program graduates, NLEN mentors, and our entire network of supporters cheering you on toward success.

CRAFT YOUR PATH

Life after the program

As a graduate of Craft Your Path, you will always have access to NLEN’s services such as resume building, interview preparation, and effective job search strategies. Join us to gain the confidence and tools you need to secure your dream job.

Upon graduation, you’re eligible for a number of opportunities, including:

  • Unsubsidized job placement with major employers across the City of Chicago
  • Enrollment in Moving Forward, NLEN’s customized skills training program for transportation, distribution, and logistics
  • Opportunity to enroll in WIOA-funded programs for continued career development
  • One-on-one support from NLEN’s Career Solutions Specialists to help navigate job searches and professional growth
  • Continued financial coaching through the Financial Opportunity Center (FOC) to improve credit, build savings, and plan for financial success
  • Invitations to NLEN networking events, job fairs, and professional development workshops
