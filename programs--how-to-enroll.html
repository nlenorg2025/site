GET STARTED

How to enroll
with NLEN

Take advantage of our award-winning job readiness training and placement programs, support services, and our digital and financial education resources.

Welcome to your next chapter.

Enrollment is your first step toward joining the North Lawndale Employment Network (NLEN). Whether you’re looking to gain new skills, find stable employment, or take control of your financial future, we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Step 1

Complete an NLEN Intake Form

Completing an NLEN Intake Form helps us learn about you, your needs, and how we can help.

Start Your Intake Form

Questions? For more information, please contact NLEN's outreach staff at outreach@nlen.org.

Step 2

Attend an Information Session

Attend one of our information sessions to learn more about our programs and services leading to long-term personal, professional, and financial empowerment.

  • Who

    Anyone interested in NLEN’s programs, services, or resources who has completed an Intake Form

  • When

    Mondays at 10 a.m.

    Thursdays at 12 p.m.

  • Where

    North Lawndale Employment Network
    1111 S. Homan Ave.
    Chicago, IL 60624

  • What To Bring

    • Birth Certificate
    • Social Security Card
    • Illinois-issued State ID

See You Soon!

There’s no need to RSVP for an information session. Just show up!

Step 3

Complete an Enrollment Application

Once you’ve attended an information session and know more about our programs, it’s time to apply for the one that’s the best fit.

There are 2 ways to complete the Enrollment Application:

  • In-Person

    After attending information session

    If you have time, you’re invited to complete your Enrollment Application in person directly after your information session. Please bring the following personal documents with you:

    • Birth Certificate
    • Social Security Card
    • Illinois-issued State ID

  • Remotely

    On your own time

    If you don’t have your personal documents with you at your information session, or would prefer to complete your Enrollment Application on your own time, you’re welcome to do so.

    When it’s complete, you must physically return it to:

    North Lawndale Employment Center
    1111 S. Homan Avenue
    Chicago, IL 60624

    Be sure to bring your personal documents with you to complete the application.

If you need assistance gathering required documents, our staff can help. Email outreach@nlen.org.

Note: Our enrollment process includes a drug screening.

Step 4

Personal Assessment

As part of our intake process, we ask a few questions to better understand where you are in your employment journey, and how we can support you along the way.

Start Your Intake Form

Questions? For more information, please contact NLEN's outreach staff at outreach@nlen.org.
