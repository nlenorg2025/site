OUR MISSION

The North Lawndale Employment Network’s mission is to improve the earnings potential of the North Lawndale community through innovative employment initiatives that lead to economic advancement and an improved quality of life.

OUR IMPACT

Improving lives through training and employment opportunities

Given the high rate of poverty resulting from racial and economic wealth disparities, we serve the unemployed and asset-limited, income-constrained employed populations in North Lawndale. We help them to secure employment within one year.
