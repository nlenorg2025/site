Spaces that inspire change

We love sharing our love letter to North Lawndale with the community. Whether planning a business meeting, celebration, corporate retreat, or workshop, you’ll find the perfect setting on our campus.

Flexible. Beautiful. Community-Rooted.

Our campus features a variety of bright, friendly spaces with state-of-the-art technology for hybrid events or presentations.

beelove café

A warm, modern space ideal for small receptions, networking events, and intimate gatherings.

  • Cozy seating
  • Welcoming atmosphere
  • Access to food & beverage options

Capacity: Up to 30 guests

Michael Scott Sr. Events Center

A flexible, light-filled venue ideal for mid-sized gatherings, workshops, receptions, and community events.

  • Built-in A/V
  • Flexible seating configurations
  • Adjacent to Steans Peace Garden

Capacity: 40-60 guests

Training Rooms

Bright, professional, tech-equipped rooms perfect for workshops, team trainings, seminars, and breakout sessions.

  • Built-in A/V & high-speed wifi
  • On-site tech support
  • Flexible seating configurations

Capacity: 10-40 guests

The Harrison Steans Peace Garden

Calm, beautifully landscaped outdoor space ideal for intimate gatherings, wellness events, and quiet reflection.

  • Native plants and foliage
  • Shaded seating
  • Peaceful, welcoming atmosphere

Capacity: TK guests

NLEN Boardroom

Polished, private setting ideal for executive meetings, strategy sessions, and small group discussions.

  • Built-in A/V
  • Video conferencing technology
  • Large conference table

Capacity: 12 guests
