Sweet Beginnings

Established in 2008, Sweet Beginnings is NLEN’s social impact business, headquartered on our North Lawndale campus. The business has hired more than 850 people with criminal records, and has received national recognition for all of the good it’s doing in our community — and beyond.

SOCIAL IMPACT BUSINESS

A Chicago-based enterprise rooted in opportunity

Sweet Beginnings, LLC, is a wholly owned subsidiary of NLEN that provides paid transitional employment to individuals returning from incarceration and those facing barriers to employment. Through the production and sales of beelove®—a line of all-natural honey and skincare products—Sweet Beginnings creates real opportunities for individuals rebuilding their lives.

Participants gain meaningful job experience while contributing to a sustainable business rooted in care — for people, community, and the environment.

At Sweet Beginnings, team members:

  • Care for urban apiaries (bee farms) across Chicagoland, including North Lawndale, O’Hare Airport, Roosevelt University’s Schaumburg campus, and the Forest Preserve District of Cook County
  • Harvest honey and produce beelove® skin and body care products
  • Learn product development, packaging, inventory management, and quality control
  • Gain hands-on experience in retail, including order fulfillment and customer service
  • Develop essential workplace skills such as punctuality, professionalism, teamwork, and communication
  • Prepare for long-term employment through consistent mentorship and coaching

Sweet Beginnings isn’t just work. It’s the first step in a lifelong transformation.

SWEET BEGINNINGS MISSION

A mission that delivers on three fronts

As a social enterprise business of NLEN, Sweet Beginnings has a triple-bottom line: people, product, and planet. Sweet Beginnings creates transitional job opportunities while producing high-quality, natural skincare products under the beelove® brand. Its impact is measured in more than profits. It’s measured in lives changed, jobs created, and community renewal.

  • Economic Revitalization

    Creating sustainable jobs that contribute to North Lawndale’s renewal.

  • Social Impact

    Providing individuals with criminal records a real chance to build a work history and reenter the workforce with confidence.

  • Quality Product

    Producing and selling all-natural honey and honey-infused skincare products that are as good for your skin as they are for the community.
