Careers at NLEN

We’re always looking for compassionate, talented people to join our growing team.

JOIN OUR TEAM

Creating a community that works.

We’re a team of changemakers, mentors, and innovators working together to reduce unemployment, support returning citizens, and close the racial wealth gap — one job at a time. With programs that span job readiness, financial empowerment, transitional employment, and entrepreneurship, our work is deeply rooted in community and powered by purpose.

Why work at NLEN?

  • Mission-driven culture: Be part of a team that believes in fair chances and long-term impact.
  • Collaborative environment: We support one another, grow together, and celebrate each win — big or small.
  • Professional growth: We invest in your development with training, mentorship, and opportunities to lead.
  • Meaningful work: Whether you’re coaching clients, managing programs, or supporting operations, you’ll be changing lives daily.
