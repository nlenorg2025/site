Donate today!

Your investment ensures the most vulnerable residents gain skills and confidence to support their families, and move towards economic prosperity.

Change lives in North Lawndale and beyond.

NLEN is honored to help the most vulnerable residents gain skills and confidence to support their families, and move towards economic prosperity.

Your investment makes it possible for individuals with barriers to employment to move forward on their pathway to self-sufficiency and brighter futures.

Each donation made to NLEN generates returns to society in the form of:

  • Taxable wages and benefits
  • Reduced dependence on food assistance, housing subsidies, and other aid programs
  • Lower rates of recidivism, which means fewer property crimes, as well as reduced trial costs and prison/jail operating costs
