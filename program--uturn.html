OUR PROGRAMS

U-Turn Permitted

U-Turn Permitted is NLEN’s 10-day job readiness training program for job-seekers, regardless of any criminal legal involvement. Participants learn essential skills such as anger management, resume building, mock interviews, and other tools for succeeding in the workplace.

Making your U-Turn

In collaboration with professional staff, U-Turn Permitted trains you to advance in the working world. The primary purpose of U-Turn Permitted is to equip you with the skills to secure and retain employment and successfully reintegrate back into society or avoid incarceration. This program focuses not just on you, the worker, but you, the individual.

U-Turn Permitted participants must:

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Pass a drug screening
  • Attend an NLEN information session
  • Be willing to commit to a 10-day job readiness program

Program features

Turn your past into possibility.

The U-Turn Permitted program is NLEN’s intensive job readiness training program designed to empower individuals facing barriers to employment, particularly those returning from incarceration or who have a heightened risk of violence engagement.

  1. Information Session Offered Mondays at 10 a.m. and Thursdays at 12 p.m. Learn more >
  2. Intake Form & Personal Assessment Can be completed directly after information session
  3. U-Turn Permitted begins! Enroll with the next available training session

  6. Professional Headshot Complimentary at the time of your graduation

U-TURN PERMITTED

Life after the program

As a graduate of U-Turn Permitted, you will always have access to NLEN’s services — no matter where your journey takes you. As you advance professionally or enter times of employment transition, we’re here to help.

Upon graduation, you’re eligible for a number of opportunities, including:

  • A transitional position with Sweet Beginnings, NLEN’s social enterprise, or other employer partners
  • Unsubsidized job placement with major employers across the City of Chicago
  • Enrollment in Moving Forward, NLEN’s customized skills training program for transportation, distribution, and logistics
  • Enrollment in our WIOA Program, which provides dedicated support and training for in-demand employment certifications for emerging adults ages 18 to 24
  • Continued financial coaching through the Financial Opportunity Center (FOC) to improve credit, build savings, and plan for financial success
  • Membership in the U-Turn Permitted Alumni Fellowship for ongoing job leads and career support
  • Invitations to NLEN networking events, job fairs, and professional development workshops
