OUR PROGRAMS

Empowered Now

90-day transitional employment with Sweet Beginnings, beelove® honey, and beelove café, offering paid jobs & career mentorship.

An immediate pathway to long-term success

Empowered Now is our transformational transitional jobs program, designed to provide meaningful, short-term, full-time employment to individuals facing significant barriers to the workforce — particularly citizens returning from incarceration.

Empowered Now participants must:

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Pass a drug screening
  • Successfully complete the U-Turn Permitted Job Readiness Training
  • Show commitment to full-time, short-term employment

Program features

Meaningful work and a fresh start

Empowered Now is more than a job-training program. It’s a structured bridge from training to stable employment. After graduating from U-Turn Permitted, you’ll be placed into one of NLEN’s social enterprise programs: Sweet Beginnings, featuring hands-on roles in beekeeping; beelove® honey, where you’ll learn to hand-craft natural skincare products; and beelove cafe, where you’ll serve customers in a vibrant, community-driven setting.

Enroll today

Transitional Employment

  • 3-month, full-time transitional employment at one of NLEN’s social impact businesses.

Skill Development

  • Job readiness, digital literacy, conflict resolution, teamwork, customer service, punctuality, productivity, and more.

Personal & Professional Development

  • One-on-one coaching, resume and interview prep, legal aid for expungement/child support, financial literacy through NLEN’s Financial Literacy Center (FOC).

Good for you, good for the community.

As a program graduate you’ll have the opportunity to work at one of our social impact businesses:

Sweet Beginnings

With work centered on beekeeping at our apiaries (bee farms) across Chicagoland, Sweet Beginnings provides rewarding, hands-on work at the intersection of nature and commerce.

beelove®

Fueled by honey harvested from our Sweet Beginnings apiaries, beelove® is our line of honey and skincare products handmade by NLEN graduates.

beelove® café

Located on NLEN’s campus, our award-winning café employs Empowered Now graduates in fulfilling customer-facing positions.

EMPOWERED NOW

Life after the program

As a graduate of Empowered Now, you will always have access to NLEN’s services such as resume building, interview preparation, and effective job search strategies. Join us to gain the confidence and tools you need as you start your new career journey.

Upon graduation, you’re eligible for a number of opportunities, including:

  • Job placement support in one of our social impact businesses
  • Enrollment in our WIOA Program, which provides dedicated support and training for in-demand employment certifications for emerging adults ages 18 to 24
  • One-on-one support from NLEN’s Career Solutions Specialists to help navigate job searches and professional growth
  • Continued financial coaching through the Financial Opportunity Center (FOC) to improve credit, build savings, and plan for financial success
  • Membership in the Empowered Now Alumni Fellowship for ongoing job leads and career support
  • Invitations to NLEN networking events, job fairs, and professional development workshops
